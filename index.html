About The Game

The Dark World is an intense survival horror game that puts you in the middle of a global zombie apocalypse. Fight for humanity's survival against endless waves of the undead.

Immersive Story

Experience a gripping narrative of survival, loss, and hope in a world consumed by darkness.

Action-Packed Gameplay

Engage in intense combat with a variety of weapons against countless zombie variants.

Multiplayer Co-op

Team up with friends or other survivors to take on the horde together.

The Story

Day 0

The Beginning

It started like any other day. People going to work, children going to school, life continuing as normal. Then came the news reports - a strange virus spreading across the globe.

Day 7

The Collapse

Within days, the world as we knew it had ended. Governments fell, cities burned, and the dead began to rise. The infected don't just want to kill you - they want to make you one of them.

Day 30

The Resistance

Humanity didn't go down without a fight. Survivors banded together, establishing hidden enclaves and resistance cells. You are one of these survivors.

Present

Your Story

You are a survivor. One of the few who managed to escape the initial outbreak. You've seen friends become enemies, watched cities fall, and learned that the only rule in this new world is survive or become them.

The Horde

Know your enemy. Every variant brings new challenges.

Walkers

Basic

The common infected. Slow but relentless. One bite is all it takes to join the horde.

  • Low Damage
  • Slow Speed
  • Low Health

Runners

Dangerous

Fast and aggressive. They hunt in packs and strike without warning.

  • Medium Damage
  • Very Fast
  • Low Health

Tanks

Deadly

Massive and powerful. Takes multiple hits to bring down. Their embrace is fatal.

  • Extreme Damage
  • Slow Speed
  • Very High Health

Crawlers

Tricky

Move on all fours and can squeeze through tight spaces. Hard to hit but fragile.

  • Low Damage
  • Fast Speed
  • Very Low Health

Spitters

Hazardous

Projectile attackers that can hit you from distance with toxic acid.

  • Ranged Attack
  • Medium Speed
  • Medium Health

Mutators

Nightmare

Highly mutated creatures with unique abilities. The nightmare versions of the infected.

  • High Damage
  • Variable Speed
  • High Health

Gameplay Features

Wave-Based Survival

Fight endless waves of zombies that increase in difficulty. How long can you survive?

Weapon Arsenal

Collect and upgrade a wide variety of weapons from pistols to heavy artillery.

Resource Scavenging

Search for supplies, ammo, and upgrades to survive longer in the apocalyptic world.

Character Progression

Level up your survivor, unlock new skills, and become a force against the horde.

Base Building

Establish and fortify safe zones to rest, craft, and prepare for the next wave.

Achievements

Complete challenges and earn rewards that showcase your survival skills.
