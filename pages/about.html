THE APOCALYPSE

Humanity's last stand begins NOW

THE STORY

It started like any other day. People going to work, children going to school, life continuing as normal. Then came the news reports - a strange virus spreading across the globe. Within days, the world as we knew it had ended.

The infected don't just want to kill you - they want to make you one of them. Every bite, every scratch brings you closer to joining the endless horde. But humanity didn't go down without a fight.

You are a survivor. One of the few who managed to escape the initial outbreak. You've seen friends become enemies, watched cities fall, and learned that the only rule in this new world is survive or become them.

YOUR MISSION

PRIMARY OBJECTIVE

Survive endless waves of undead horde. Each wave brings more zombies, faster speeds, and deadlier variants. How long can you last?

SECONDARY OBJECTIVE

Scavenge for supplies, weapons, and resources. Every survivor counts in this fight for humanity's future.

TEAM OBJECTIVE

Form alliances with other survivors. Coordinate attacks, share resources, and watch each other's backs.
